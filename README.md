# Boosting-Customer-Conversion-via-Image-Preference-AI-Chatbots
Converting browsers to buyers: An AI-driven chatbot that gains real-time preference insights from image interactions to provide targeted help.
The Problem (Why we built this?)
Most websites lose customers because of boring forms. People hate typing their phone numbers or house sizes. They just want to see beautiful designs.

✨ Our Solution (The "Wow" Strategy)
We use AI-Driven insights to catch customer preferences without asking questions.

Browse: A customer looks at an image of a "Modern Style" living room.

Recognize: The system "sees" the click and identifies their preference instantly.

Chat: The chatbot pops up with targeted help: "I see you love Modern Style! Want to see a 3D plan for your home?"

🎥 Live Demo (Watch AI in action)
User clicks a style -> AI instantly recognizes the preference -> Chatbot offers targeted help.

🛠️ How it Works (Lightweight & Fast)
We used a Lightweight No-Code/Low-Code stack to build this professional tool:

1. The Brain (Coze AI)
We created an AI Agent that can "remember" variables.

Variable: user_style_pref (Stores what the customer likes).

Logic: The AI changes its greeting based on this variable.

Figure 1: Our AI strategy for recognizing user preferences.

2. The Website (Framer)
A fast, high-end landing page. Every image has a "hidden listener" that tells the AI what the customer is looking at.

3. The Integration (JS SDK)
We used a simple script to connect the website and the AI. It passes data in real-time to boost purchase intent.

Higher Conversion: No more friction. Customers start talking to AI naturally.

Better Leads: You know exactly what the customer wants before the first "Hello".
